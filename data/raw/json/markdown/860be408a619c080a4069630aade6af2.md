## Pay-As-You-Go Pricing:
When you use more than your monthly allowance, additional usage is deducted from your prepaid wallet balance at these rates: 
🎨
Images:
$1 per 100 additional HD images ($0.01 each), 12 Genius images ($0.08 each), or 4 Super Genius 2K images ($0.25 each)
💬
Chat:
$1 per 350 additional messages (≈ $0.003 each) or 50 Genius messages ($0.02 each)
🎬
Video:
$0.20 per additional second of standard video generation, or $2.50 per Hollywood video
🎵
Music:
$1 per 20 additional songs ($0.05 each)
**Important:** You need to maintain a positive wallet balance to continue using services. Add funds manually or enable auto top-up to automatically recharge when your balance gets low.
### Example:
If you generate 600 HD images in a month:
  * First 500 are free (included in your Pro subscription)
  * Extra 100 HD images cost $1, deducted from your wallet balance
  * You can add funds manually or enable auto top-up in your dashboard
